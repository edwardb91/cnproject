Square Wheel Press

In 2020 I launched my own independent digital press to pursue my passions for creative writing and literary publishing. Square Wheel Press publishes free digital anthologies of author's writings on a selected theme; containing poetry, short fiction, and non-fiction. As Editor-in-Chief, I oversee all stages of publishing process and undertake editing and proofreading responsibilities along with our other editor. I am also the typesetter for the anthologies, bringing our author's amazing work to life on paper.

Square Wheel Press is currently accepting submissions for its second anthology edition. Prospective authors should visit Square Wheel Press' website for further information on the theme and submission guidelines.
