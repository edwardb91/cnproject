During my MA in Publishing at UCL I undertook a voluntary position as the typesetter for the academic journal Interscript. I was responsible for creating the template for all articles, as well as producing the final PDF files for publication. The articles that I worked on directly can be found here. The template I produce for the journal is still in use for all current issues.
